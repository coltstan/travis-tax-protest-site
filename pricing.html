Questions

Common questions

What does it cost?

Do it yourself for a flat $49 per property and keep 100% of the savings, or let us handle it for 30% of your first-year savings — and nothing at all if we don't reduce your bill.

What if you don't save me money?

On the done-for-you plan you pay $0. We only earn when you do — no upfront fee, no risk.

How do you know my home is over-appraised?

We compare it to similar homes the county itself values nearby, adjust each for differences (size, age, quality, condition, baths, garage, pool, land), and check it against the Texas "equal & uniform" standard. You see the full comparison.

Do you file it and go to the hearing?

Yes — on done-for-you we prepare and file your Notice of Protest and represent you through the hearing. On the DIY plan, we hand you the ready-to-file form.

When's the deadline?

May 15, or 30 days after your appraisal notice is mailed — whichever is later. Start early so there's time to prepare.
