How it works

Simple, contingency-based terms — the same model the big firms use, at a lower rate.

  • You pay nothing upfront. No fee unless we reduce your appraised value.
  • Our fee is 30% of your first-year tax savings — less than the 35–50% most Texas firms charge.
  • We handle it end to end — build the comparable-homes case, file your Notice of Protest, and represent you through the hearing.
  • You authorize us as your agent for this protest (Texas Comptroller Form 50-162) — we'll send it to e-sign once we confirm your case.
  • You're never locked in. Cancel any time before we file.
